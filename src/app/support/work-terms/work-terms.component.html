Return Policy(by Online Shopping Cart)

Online Shopping Cart’s return & cancellation policy gives you an option to cancel or return the products in case of damage within the specified return / cancellation period. We only ask you not to use the product in case you want to return it and also to preserve its original condition, tags and packaging. You are always welcome to try the product on, but make sure that you are not causing any kind of damage to the product as well as the package.

Return Policy

We work with the motto of customer satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with the product or service we have provided, our customers are always welcome to return the products and request refunds from us. Our policy EXCEPTIONS and RULES are as following:

  • We take returns or exchanges on products that are defective, wrong size or delivered a wrong product.
  • All items to be returned or exchanged must be unused and in their original condition with all original tags and packaging intact.
  • If you self-ship your returns, please make sure that the packaging is intact and secured to prevent any kind of loss or damage during transit.
  • If you self-ship the product a reimbursement fee of Rs. 100 will be paid to the customer as courier charges if necessary proof is provided.
  • For self-ship returns the refund for returned products will only be initiated if their quality is intact when at the warehouse. If the quality check fails the product will be reshipped back to you.
  • If the payment for the purchased product is made by credit card, refunds will be issued to the original credit card provided at the time of purchase and in case of payment gateway name payments refund will be made to the same account.
How to return a product?

You can return products purchased from Online Shopping Cart within the specified return/exchange period mentioned with the product at the time of buying by making a return request from the account which the product is been bought.

  • Create a Return Request

    To create a return request you will need to click on the WhatsApp icon on the website. It will redirect you to Online Shopping Cart’s business chat and you can make the return request there with proof. Details you have to send in WhatsApp for returning the product are:

    • A video of you opening the courier for the first time. (The video should be continues with no blackout or edits in between)
    • Reason for the return.
  • Verification

    Our team will verify your return request and video. After verification you will be directed with the shipping address to which you can return the product.

  • Self-Shipping

    Customer is requested to self-ship the product back to the address that is provided by the Online Shopping Cart team.

  • Return Acknowledgement

    After receiving the returned product at our side, we will run a quality check over the returned product. If the returned product passes the quality check, the price of the returned product is credited back to the customer.
